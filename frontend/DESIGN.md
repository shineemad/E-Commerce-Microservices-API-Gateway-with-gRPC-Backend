# Design System: ShopGo

## Visual Personality
- **Style:** Clean, Modern, Minimalist
- **Core Aesthetic:** Product-focused with generous whitespace and soft container styling.
- **Tone:** Professional, Accessible, High-tech yet friendly.

## Color Palette
### Primary Colors
- **Charcoal/Black:** `#1A1A1A` (Primary text, main buttons, footer background)
- **Pure White:** `#FFFFFF` (Main background, card backgrounds)
- **Soft Gray:** `#F5F5F5` (Secondary backgrounds, input fields)

### Secondary/UI Colors
- **Accent Red:** `#E74C3C` (Notification badges, discount tags)
- **Border Gray:** `#E0E0E0` (Dividers, card borders)
- **Muted Text:** `#757575` (Reviews, category counts, secondary info)
- **Star Gold:** `#F1C40F` (Rating stars)

## Typography
- **Primary Font:** Sans-serif (likely Inter or similar geometric sans)
- **Headings:**
  - **Display (Hero):** Bold, large scale, tight tracking.
  - **Section Headings:** Semi-bold, medium scale.
- **Body:** Regular weight for descriptions, Medium for product titles.
- **Price:** Bold, consistent placement.

## Component Patterns
### Product Cards
- **Structure:** Image container (rounded corners) > Category Label > Title > Rating > Price > Action Buttons.
- **Corner Radius:** `16px` for image containers, `8px` for buttons.
- **Shadows:** Very subtle or flat with soft borders.

### Navigation
- **Header:** Transparent or white background, centered links, right-aligned icons (Search, Cart, Profile).
- **Secondary Nav:** Sidebar categories with active state highlighting.

### Buttons
- **Primary (Buy Now):** Solid black background, white text, pill-shaped or rounded.
- **Secondary (Add to Cart):** Ghost button with border or light gray background.

## Layout & Grids
- **Grid:** 12-column system.
- **Product Grid:** 3 items per row in main view, 4 items in recommendations.
- **Spacing:** Large vertical padding between sections (`80px - 120px`).
- **Containers:** Max-width central container with generous margins.

## Brand Assets
- **Logo:** Simple wordmark "Stuffsus" with a geometric icon.
- **Imagery:** High-quality studio product photography on neutral backgrounds.
